Facts

Rewards of Vending

  1. Proving great food to New Yorkers at affordable prices.
  2. Less startup costs than a brick-and-mortar restaurant.
  3. Work outdoors, a bonus during pandemics.

Drawbacks of Vending

  1. There are only 3,100 street vending licenses available -- and 20,000 vendors!
  2. Fines can be up to $500 for non-health related infractions.
  3. Small spaces to work in.
